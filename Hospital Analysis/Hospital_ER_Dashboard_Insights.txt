1. Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a consolidated view of hospital emergency room performance, patient demographics, wait times, satisfaction levels, referral patterns, and admission outcomes.
It consists of three main report pages:
- Consolidated View (Full Overview)
- Monthly View
- Patient Details (Record-level view)

2. Key KPIs (Consolidated View)
- Total Patients: 9216
- Average Wait Time: 35.3 minutes
- Patient Satisfaction Score: 4.99
- No. of Patients Referred: 3816

3. Patient Admission Insights
- 50.04% of patients were Admitted.
- 49.96% were Not Admitted.

4. Gender Distribution
- Male patients are the majority.
- Female patients form the second-largest group.
- A very small percentage is Not Confirmed.

5. Patient Age Group Analysis
- Balanced distribution across all age groups.
- Slightly higher counts in working-age and senior categories.

6. Patients by Day
- Patient volume remains consistent throughout the week.
- Slight increase on weekends.

7. % of Patients Seen Within 30 Minutes
- 59.32% were seen within target time.
- 40.68% were seen after 30 minutes.

8. Top 5 Department Referrals
- General Practice and Orthopedics receive the highest referrals.
- Physiotherapy and Cardiology see fewer referrals.

9. Patient Race Distribution
- Highest volumes from White and African American groups.
- Asian, Pacific Islander, and mixed-race groups follow.

10. Monthly View Insights
- Trends change dynamically based on Year and Month slicers.
- Helps detect seasonal or monthly shifts in ER patterns.

11. Patient Details Page
- Complete record-level view for all patients.
- Includes race, referral department, wait-time, admission status, etc.

12. Summary
This dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of ER operations, helping with:
- Patient flow management
- Staffing and scheduling
- Operational efficiency
- Demographic-based decision making
