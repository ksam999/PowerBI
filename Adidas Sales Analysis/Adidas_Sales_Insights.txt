Adidas Sales Analysis – Insights

1. Overall Performance
- Total Sales: $900M
- Total Profit: $332.1M
- Total Units Sold: 2M
- Average Margin: 36.91%
- Average Price per Unit: $45.2
The business shows strong profitability with healthy margins and high-volume sales.

2. Monthly Sales Trend
- Peak months: July ($95M), August ($92M), December ($86M)
- Lowest month: March ($57M)
Sales rise during mid-year and the holiday season, showing clear seasonal demand patterns.

3. Daily Sales Trend
- Highest sales day: Friday ($147M)
- Decline observed toward Monday ($112M)
Weekends and Fridays contribute the highest revenue, indicating strong end-of-week sales behavior.

4. Sales by Product Category
Top-performing product categories:
- Men’s Streetwear
- Women’s Apparel
- Men’s Athletic Wear
- Women’s Streetwear
Men’s and women’s apparel dominate overall product sales.

5. Sales by Region
Regional sales distribution:
- West: 30%
- Northeast: 21%
- Southeast: 18%
- South: 16%
- Midwest: 15%
The West region is the strongest market for Adidas.

6. Sales by Retailer
Top retailers contributing to revenue:
- West Gear
- Foot Locker
- Sports Direct
- Kohl’s
- Amazon
These retailers form the core sales channels, with West Gear contributing the largest share.

7. Sales by State
Sales are widely distributed across the U.S., with stronger performance in western and northeastern states.

Conclusion
The Adidas Sales Analysis indicates strong financial performance driven by key product categories, high-performing regions, and top retailers. Seasonal peaks and weekly trends offer insights that can guide marketing, promotions, and inventory management strategies.
